CompTIA Project+

The CompTIA Project+ certification is designed for IT professionals who manage small-to-medium-sized projects. It covers the entire project lifecycle, from initiation and planning to execution, monitoring, and closing. Here's an overview of what you need to know:

Key Topics Covered

  • Project Basics: Understanding project management concepts, methodologies, and frameworks.
  • Project Constraints: Managing scope, time, cost, quality, resources, and risks.
  • Communication and Change Management: Effective communication strategies and managing change during projects.
  • Tools and Documentation: Using project management tools and maintaining proper documentation.
