Axelos - ITIL Foundation

The ITIL (Information Technology Infrastructure Library) Foundation certification is an entry-level qualification that introduces you to the ITIL framework, which is widely used for IT service management (ITSM). Here's what you need to know:

Key Concepts

  • Service Management: Understanding the principles of service management and the value it brings to organizations.
  • ITIL Framework: Learning the ITIL service lifecycle, including service strategy, design, transition, operation, and continual improvement.
  • Key Processes: Familiarizing yourself with key ITIL processes such as incident management, problem management, and change management.
  • Roles and Responsibilities: Understanding the roles involved in IT service management and their responsibilities.
